Tips

• There is a Product name and Product price, So here you put all your goods in your store, after you finish putting them, click Add Products to add them to your Product list.There is Search, so you can search for the product that you have added to the product list.

• In the Product listed, there are Add to cart buttons, Edit buttons, Delete buttons, In the Add to cart button, click on it if that's what the customer selected. In the Edit button, you can edit the product name (if you like) and Price if there is a price change. On the Delete button, if you want to delete the product.

• After you click on Add to cart, it will go directly to the Selected Products, and there is a Delete button to delete the Product and an Update button if he bought a lot of a brand. (ex. he will buy 5 Milo, so you type 5 in the update and the total of the product will be automatic. If the customer is about to pay, put it in Customers pay so you know how much you will change.

• Below, there is a Generate button if you want to create a receipt and you can also print it if you like.
